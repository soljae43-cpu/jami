Understanding Measurements

Measurement helps us know how big, long, heavy, hot, or fast something is. Without measurement, we cannot build houses, cook food, or even tell time!

There are two kinds of systems: Metric (used worldwide) and Customary (like in the US).

Metric Conversion Table

UnitSymbolEquivalent
Kilometerkm1000 meters

Centimetercm10 millimeters

The Metric System uses powers of 10. That makes conversions easy!

Conversion Using Ratios

To convert, we multiply by a conversion factor. If we know:

1 meter = 100 cm

Then: 5m √ó 100 = 500cm
Ratios help us solve life problems like height measurements and distance.
